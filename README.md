[POINT 1 — WRONG STUDY STRATEGY]
(Thoda aage aao. Jaise koi secret share kar rahe ho.)

Pehli baat. Aur ye sunne mein bohot simple lagegi but trust me ye biggest reason hai.

Wrong study strategy.

Dekho hum sab ne ye suna hai na ki "hard work is the key to success" wagairah wagairah. Aur main ye nahi bol raha ki hard work zaroori nahi hai. Obviously zaroori hai. But JEE mein sirf hard work se kaam nahi chalta yaar.

Main tumhe ek real example deta hoon. Mere ek dost the. Class 12th mein. Banda genuinely mehnat karta tha. Subah 6 baje uthta tha raat 12 baje tak padhai. Coaching bhi jaata tha. Notes bhi banata tha. Sab karta tha.

Result aaya? 89 percentile.

Aur ek aur dost tha mera. Woh honestly bolu toh utna padhta nahi tha jitna pehle wala. Like 6-7 ghante max. But banda ek kaam karta tha. Har din sone se pehle next day ka poora plan likhta tha. Ki kal mujhe ye chapter karna hai, is topic ke itne questions karne hain, revision ye karna hai.

Uska result? 99.2 percentile.

Same school. Same coaching. Same teachers. Farak kya tha? Approach ka farak tha.

(Thoda ruko. Effect aane do.)

Dekho yaar JEE itna vast syllabus hai ki agar tum randomly padhoge — aaj mood hai toh physics padh leta hoon, kal organic chemistry kar lunga — toh tum kabhi cover nahi kar paoge.

Tumhe ek system chahiye. Ek plan chahiye. Aur usse follow karne ka discipline chahiye.

Ek simple tip deta hoon jo aaj se implement kar sakte ho. Raat ko sone se pehle ek diary lo ya phone mein notes mein likho — kal ke 3 most important tasks kya hain. Bas 3. Zyada nahi. Aur next day pehle woh 3 khatam karo.

Itna sa change hai. But ye bohot kuch badal deta hai. Trust me.

(Halka smile.)

NCERT ko ignore karna.

(Serious ho jao.)

Ab sochna yaar. 60 percent paper ek aisi book se aa raha hai jo tumhe free milti hai school se. Aur tum usse padh nahi rahe ho.

Ek student ka DM padh ke sunata hoon. Usne likha tha "bhaiya maine puri Cengage solve kar li chemistry ki but mains mein 40 marks aaye." Maine pucha NCERT padhi thi? Bola "nahi wo toh basic hai."

Yaar basic hai toh isliye toh padhni chahiye na. Foundation basic hota hai. Agar foundation strong nahi hai toh building kitni bhi acchi banao gir jaayegi.

Toh please yaar NCERT ko seriously lo. Har chapter NCERT se start karo. Line by line padho. Phir reference book se depth mein jao. Aur exam se pehle NCERT wapas revise karo.

[POINT 3 — NO REVISION PLAN]
(Energy thodi build karo.)

Teesri baat. Aur ye baat science ne prove ki hai. Main koi opinion nahi de raha. Ye data hai.

Revision nahi karte tum log. Ya karte bhi ho toh randomly karte ho. Koi system nahi hai.

1885 mein ek German scientist the Hermann Ebbinghaus. Unhone ek experiment kiya memory pe. Aur unhone ek curve discover kiya jisko bolte hain Forgetting Curve.

(On screen: Forgetting Curve ka simple graph dikhao)

Basically ye curve kya kehta hai ki tum kuch naya padhte ho aaj. Ek ghante baad tumhe uska sirf 50 percent yaad rehta hai. Ek din baad 30 percent. Aur ek hafte baad? Bhai sirf 10 percent.

Matlab tum ek poora chapter padhte ho aaj. Sunday ko padha. Full din laga diya. Agle Sunday tak tumhe uska sirf 10 percent yaad hai. Phir exam se pehle tum wahi chapter dobara se scratch se padhte ho. Phir se poora din lagta hai.

Ye hai asli time waste. Bina revision ke padhna sabse bada time waste hai.

(Ab solution do.)

But acchi baat ye hai ki is problem ka solution bohot simple hai. Ek formula hai. 1-3-7-21.

Din 1 — chapter padho. Fresh learning.
Din 3 — ek quick revision karo. 20-30 minute.
Din 7 — bina notes dekhe problems solve karo.
Din 21 — ek final revision. Previous year questions try karo.

Chaar baar touch kar liya toh woh chapter pakka ho jaata hai. Permanently.

Aur jab revise karo toh pehle khud yaad karo. Book mat kholo. Socho ki is chapter mein kya kya tha. Jo yaad aaye likho. Phir book se match karo. Isko bolte hain active recall. Ye normal revision se 3 guna zyada effective hai.

Aur neend? Tumhara brain sote waqt jo tum din mein padhte ho usse process karta hai aur store karta hai. Agar tum soye nahi toh tumne din mein jo padha woh save hi nahi hoga. Jaise phone mein file download ki but save nahi ki.

7 se 8 ghante ki neend. Non negotiable.
